Top 5 Restaurants in the Pasadena Area

Top 5 Restaurants

  1. Olive Garden

  2. Canes

  3. Wingstop

  4. Cheddars

  5. Taqueria Azucena




Olive Garden

Olive Garden is my number one choice to eat at in the Pasadena Area. The different variety of exquisite foods, and the unlimited bread and salad. I know it might come out pretty expensive but it will be worth it. You can dress up nice and take your date there to get them a sweet taste of the Italy life.

Canes

Canes is my second choice to eat at in the Pasadena Area. They are known for their Cane's sauce. I think it is made by mayon and ketchup and I don't know what else. I know options are not plenty but you can't ever go wrong with chicken tenders and fries. And you can switch out your coleslaw for bread or extra fries. You can take your date here for a quick meal, after a movie, or if you are looking for something that isn't McDonalds or burger king. The prices have gone up since the recession but it is still worth it. Give it a go next time you are looking for a quick meal.



Go back to list




Wingstop

Wingstop is my number three on the list. They have more variety than meets the eye. They have chicken fingers, boneless wings, bone-in wings, and even burgers now. They have a bunch of flavors but my top is their buffalo flavor. Their fries are top tier. I think they add a bit of sugar on them and poom. So delicious. It is not that expensive and not known for it's dining experience. So it is a great food choice to go eat in the car, at the park, or while watching a movie.

Cheddars

Cheddar would had been higher on my list if it wasn't that expensive. They have alot of variety when it comes to food and drinks. They offer vegan options, seafood, and delicious desserts. After sitting down they always bring you a crossaint that has just the right amount of butter and something else that makes the crossaint have a pleasant flavor. It is more on the pricey side because of their upsells. So much food that you can buy that if you aren't carefull you can spend easily $100. This is a great spot for a date, or after doing stuff. Sometimes the wait to get your food takes a bit because of the amount of people that go there, but that just means more time to talk. Give it a shot on a Friday night after work.

Taqueria Azucena

This is one of the top hidden restaurants in the Pasadena Area. You have probably never been. I only went because my girl's sister told us about it. And we were not dissapointed. It is a Mexican restaurant so they sell a lot of tacos, burgers, and tortas. They are as expensive as fast food places like McDonalds or Burger King. But more authentic food than them. Their exterior is not really used for dining at their spot. The food truck is in a trailer park area. So itt is more of a pick up your food and leave, or get in your car and eat. When you are looking for something fast, and with great test, and not trying to eat at your typical fast food chain, then Taqueria Azucena is for you. Try it out. Just make sure to call them ahead of time to place your order since they do have a bunch of line in the afternoons.
